Colors

Our colors are vibrant, exciting and communicate freshness, fun and youth. They are meant to be used as accents and calls to action, with an eye constantly on the content the design contains, making sure that it stands out more than the design itself.

Grape Drink is the main flood color, with Sprout being the attention-grabber. Blue is used to communicate interactivity, like buttons and text links. Red is used in instances of required attention, errors and irreversible, destructive actions.
